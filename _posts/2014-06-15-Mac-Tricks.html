Share Internet via Wifi

"Internet Sharing allows other computers to share your connection to the Internet."
前提: Mac用Ethernet X接入互联网
  1. 打开System Preferences/Sharing
  2. 在左侧列表点击Internet Sharing，先不勾选
  3. 在右侧Share your connection from 选择接入互联网的网卡
  4. To computers using 勾选Wi-Fi，并且在Wi-Fi Options...输入Network Name和Password等信息
  5. 勾选左侧的Interneet Sharing
